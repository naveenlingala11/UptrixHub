Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Uptrix Hub uses cookies and similar technologies in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India).

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve functionality, security, and user experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login, session management, and security
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand usage patterns

3. Purpose of Cookies

  • Maintain authenticated sessions
  • Improve performance
  • Prevent fraud and misuse
  • Analyze platform usage

4. Consent

By clicking “Accept” on our cookie banner, you provide explicit consent as required under Indian DPDP law.

5. Managing Cookies

You may disable cookies through browser settings, however this may impact platform functionality.
