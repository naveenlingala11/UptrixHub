Data Protection & GDPR Compliance

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Overview

Uptrix Hub complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India).

2. Lawful Basis for Processing

  • User consent
  • Contractual necessity
  • Legal obligations
  • Legitimate interests

3. Rights of Users

  • Right to access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict processing

4. Cross-Border Transfers

Data may be processed outside India or the EU using secure, legally compliant infrastructure.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained only for lawful and operational purposes.
